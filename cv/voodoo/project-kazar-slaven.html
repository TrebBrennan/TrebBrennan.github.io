Kazar Slaven

Brand design and website

Kazar Slaven, a privately owned Chartered Accounting and Insolvency practice in Canberra. Had been trading for a number of years with only a logo as their primary piece of brand collateral. Voodoo introduced an adjusted logo and colour scheme to the brand that kept some elements from their old identity, for recognisability. But moved away from such an austere look, towards one which better suited Kazar Slaven’s helpful and positive approach to working with clients.
