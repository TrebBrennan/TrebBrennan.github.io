Branding.

“Great brands don’t just happen. Developing a powerful brand is both a creative and strategic exercise that involves appraising every aspect of a business (or offering) and how it needs to be experienced”

As a specialist branding agency our services cover both the strategic and creative aspects of brand development. We work with clients through the process of identifying important intangible aspects of branding such as positioning, essence, values and tone. These aspects underpin design of the visual components of an engaging brand such as the logo, brand mark, typeface, colour palette and visual identity. Your brand should tell a story and invoke the desired response from a clearly identified audience.

Services include

  • Brand strategy and positioning
  • Corporate identity generation
  • Logo and brand mark design
  • Visual identity design
  • Brand collateral design
  • Style guide development

Digital solutions

“It would appear that we have reached the limits of what it is possible to achieve with computer technology, although one should be careful with such statements, as they tend to sound pretty silly in 5 years” —  John von Neumann

Our digital services include design and technical solutions for web and mobile. We embrace the fast-paced, ever-evolving nature of the digital realm and enjoy the challenge of discovering new trends and technologies. We employ a user-centric approach to the design of websites and applications to ensure that they effectively engage with users. We have provided consulting, user-interface design, compliance, usability and front-end development services to federal government departments and large private sector clients. Our development services include Adobe Business Catalyst, WordPress, eCommerce, and custom web applications.

Services include

  • User-experience consulting
  • User-interface design
  • Information architecture mapping and strategy
  • Content management system (CMS) development, integration and deployment
  • eCommerce solutions
  • Content conversion
  • Usability and accessibility testing
  • W3C and WCAG 2.0 compliance
  • Email marketing
  • Search engine marketing such as Google adwords
  • Custom PHP web application development
  • WordPress development
