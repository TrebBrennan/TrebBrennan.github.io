Voodoo Web Online Privacy Policy

Voodoo Web is totally committed to maintaining your privacy. We believe that you have the right to know how we deal with any personal information you submit to us either through our staff or through this web site.

Personal Information Collected Online

The only information we collect online is that which is submitted by you for the sole purpose of facilitating an order with us. No information is passed on or shared with any other person or organisation, other than any shipping information if required to consign an order.

Computer Tracking and Cookies

Our web site is not set up to track, collect or distribute any personal information not entered by visitors. Our site logs do generate certain kinds of non-identifying site usage data, such as the number of visitors; operating system and browser used; and the pages that are visited. This information is used solely for internal purposes to improve our web site, but again, the statistics contain no personal information and cannot be used to gather such information.
