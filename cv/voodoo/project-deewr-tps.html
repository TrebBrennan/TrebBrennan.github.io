Australian Education International

Tuition Protection Service - user-interface design, W3C and WCAG compliant HTML, responsive web design

Brief

Australian Education International (AEI) approached Voodoo Creative to create the design and front-end development of their Tuition Protection Service (TPS) website. The project presented a very tight timeline for the work to be completed, in order for the AEI to meet internal deadlines.

Solution

Voodoo considered the target audience of TPS, being a young demographic who are more likely to view internet resources on mobile devices, when deciding to use a responsive web design solution that would provide a well-rounded experience for any sized device. In developing the concept, Voodoo chose a simplified design to accommodate clean, easy to manage code which the AEI development team could easily maintain over the long term.
