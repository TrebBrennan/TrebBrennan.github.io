Branding and design services

Coca Cola, Nike, Disney, Google. They epitomise strong, enduring and powerful brands. Their recognisability, what they stand for, and the way they resonate with consumers drives their success and provides value which, when quantified, is simply staggering. Take, for example, Coca Cola, whose brand value is estimated at approximately $70 billion. The importance and value of branding is clear.

At Voodoo, we take a strategic approach to branding and offer a broad range of branding services, from basic branding packages and logo design, right through to research and corporate identity generation.

Services include

  • Brand strategy
  • Brand research
  • Brand positioning
  • Corporate identity generation
  • Logo design
  • Brand collateral
  • Style guide development
