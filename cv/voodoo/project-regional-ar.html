Department of Regional Australia, Regional Development and Local Government

Annual Report 2010-11

Overview

The Department desired a modern, contemporary design and layout for their inaugural annual report that would effectively showcase the projects and work being undertaken across Australia. Using the Departmental corporate colour palette as a basis, Voodoo worked closely with key stakeholders to achieve a design and layout that met the objectives for a clean aesthetic through clever font choices and use of design elements. Being a newly formed Department, a number of challenges were encountered throughout the project, including internal resourcing issues and scope changes. Despite the timeframe pressure that these challenges created, Voodoo’s flexibility and effective project management ensured that key deadlines were met. The hardcopy version of the Annual Report was complemented by an accessible, simple to use online version and accessible PDF. The Annual Report was well received by both internal and external stakeholders, and the project team was extremely grateful for Voodoo’s efforts, as the below message is testament to.
