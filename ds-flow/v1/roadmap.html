Roadmap

Our roadmaps are how we share with you where we’re headed.

The Design System roadmaps are released per annual quarter as Q1, Q2, Q3 and Q4. They let you know what we’re working on now and what’s coming next.

That’s where you come in -- because it’s the community of developers and designers like you who help influence our direction.

The answer to ‘Are we there yet?’ is ‘Where do you want to go?’

 

Roadmap Q3 2017

Technology

The technology supports mobile and responsive design. There a currently three flavours to download: React, JQuery and JavaScript.

 

DONE

The code for NPM will be improved.

Mobile testing of the system for android and apple devices.

The fukol-grids framework will be added to provide CSS grid layouts.

 

Components

The components are progressed through three stages of developement: New, In progress and Live. Each stage develops a variety of aspects to a high level of standards, accessiblity and usability.

 

Accessibility testing for all components migrating to live.

Training articles and resources for how to contribute.

Introduce the secure forms pattern.

 

Community

The community supports our design system through a federated approach to learning, contributing and discussing on a continuous basis.

 

Create a monthly event for Canberra and Sydney.

Celebrate the community champion of the month for each month.

Meet with one new agency per month to discuss the design system.
