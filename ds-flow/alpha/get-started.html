Getting started

We have set up different pathways to get you started quickly. Which is best for you? It all depends on your project needs and technical understanding. And whether you're a developer or a designer.

Developers

Start with some of the following:

  1. Familiarise yourself with our markup and style guidelines
  2. Review our Components, which can also be customised to meet your specific project needs. Of course, you may just choose to build your own.
  3. Install the latest Production Components distributed through the npm ecosystem.

Designers

Start with some of the following:

  1. Explore the x guide to learn about our design patterns and principles.
  2. Review our Templates to familiarise yourself with templates that you can use to kickstart your design.
  3. Review our Components to familiarise yourself with the components that you can incorporate into your designs.
  4. Try out our CDN links for prototyping and rapid testing.
